## The Basics: Your First Shortened URL

When you land on MiniFyn's homepage, you'll notice our clean, focused interface. The URL shortener is front and center - simply paste your long URL into the input box and click "Shorten". But there's more to it than meets the eye.

* Print materials and business cards
    
* Restaurant menus
